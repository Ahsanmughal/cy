Introduction

Cynax is using artificial intelligence and machine learning to improve the intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance capaabilities. The approch is to synthesize reams of data into actionable intelligence and accurate targetting information at speed and scale in high-risk environments.

Drone Academy

CYNAX has now 10 years in Unmanned Aircraft operation, in-house flight instructors, a purpose built drone fleet and industry relations in the civilian and miltiary field. At CYNAX Flight Acadmey, we help you increase your mission readiness in high-threat environments. Our flexible defense and government service solutions are designed to ensure you are ready to go now - when it counts.

At our flight Academy, cadets train in various mission areas such as intelligence, Surveillance, Target Acquistion, and Reconnaissance. We offer extended and curated programs for specific concept of operations (CONOPS) to execute Maritime Surveillance mission operations and enhance mission effectiveness

Military Training

CYNAX'S mission facilitates the ability to educate, train and inspire the future generation of aviators while upholding the high values of duty and preparing cadets for a career of professional excellence.

CYNAX academy offers virtual and aygmented reality training courses. Preparing pilots for situations to ensure the security of personnel and the success of their assignments or to satisfy the increasing demand of the industry including training with variety of drones that would otherwise be unavailable.

Civilian Training

Cynax's civilian drone academy facilitates training in unmanned systems using our continuously advancing technology and origressive curriculum qualifying cadets to uphold the values of diligence, accountabiliyy and safety in the highest regard.
