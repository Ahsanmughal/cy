WHAT IS AIPOD?

  • AiPOD which is designed to more intelligently acquire and adapt data for the perception system.

  • This advancement in CYNAX technology further strengthens its biomimicry approach to visual perception, essentially enabling optical devices to see and perceive more to better evaluate potential hazards and alert dangerous situations.

  • This advancement in AiNanoLab technology further strengthens its biomimicry approach to visual perception, essentially enabling optical devices to see and perceive more to better evaluate potential hazards and alert dangerous situations.

  • AiPOD is a groundbreaking form of Artificial perception that fuses LiDAR, FLIR, Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence to deliver unprecedented advancements in perception and surveillance.

  • AiPOD combines an onboard Ai processor, HD, Dual-Sensor Thermal Camera and processed data target, producing a Personal Risk Score(PRS) or threat indicator based on convolutional assessments between sentiment analysis and Govt data.

  • AiPOD can also be connected to larger mesh networks using onboard 4G/5G modems for increased coverage.

Weapon Detection

  • Using the most advanced computer vision technology giving our system the ability to detect and recognize the weapon's model in real time.

  • The system utilizes multiple sensors in one gimbal: MMW/MWIR and CV which sit on top of multiple circuit boards (PCB) fused with an AI-CORE library packed with pre-trained signatures of detection targets.

  • First PCB is responsible for analog to digital and digital to analog conversions of the video stream. The second board is the main processing unit realizing the presented fast image fusion algorithm

Narcotics Spectral Signature Detection & Recognition

  • AiPOD NARCOTIC DETECTION SYSTEM (NDS) highlights classified drugs based on score imagery computed from selected wavelengths based on the chemical and electromagnetic signatures.

  • AiPOD uses a combined heterogeneous anomaly detection using Hyperspectral sensors with a deep learning classifier. Anomaly detection initially extracts suspect stain patterns. A classifier then categorizes anomalies as drug or non-drug stain patterns.

  • Designed for law enforcement, correctional and customs use during screening or at checkpoints , the AiPOD Narcotics Detection (AND) device detects all major classes of narcotics in seconds including opiates, cocaine- and amphetamine-based drugs.
