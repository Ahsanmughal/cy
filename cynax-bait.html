Enhanced ISR Awareness

BAiT is the latest airborne reconnaissance solution in the form of Nano-UAV from AiNanoLab.

Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance

In a class of its own, BAiT offers next generation capabilities for Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR). Weighing approximately 60 grams, combined with a minimalistic form factor, it enhances the dismounted foot soldier whose protection and offensive capability is largely limited to what can be carried or worn. BAiT acts as a force multiplier and provides unprecedented tactical situational awareness.

Artificial Intelligence Data Visualization

BAiT is equipped with EO and IR technology, combined with onboard artificial intelligence to provide the end user with next gen capabilities facilitating real time facial recognition & identification, autonomous flight in both dynamic outdoor environments and indoor GPS denied environments. The onboard AI module optimizes data collection, enabling it to transfer less data at a higher quality & relevance, providing rapid perception, tracing and mapping.

Ground Control Station (GCS)

BAiT can be controlled via a ground control station or allowed to make real time navigation decisions independent of GCS manual control in the capacity of indoor autonomous flight, utilizing the visual feed from the onboard depth cameras combined with a Visual Processing Unit (VPU). BAiT is capable of transmitting EO and Thermal stream processed by the onboard AI from a distance up to 2.5Km to a GCS enabling live, real-time actionable intel.

The GCS enables all necessary functions to plan, execute, and analyze missions. All mission data is stored on a removeable SD-card on the base station for review. Other networked or integrated solutions for displaying mission data in real time are available.
