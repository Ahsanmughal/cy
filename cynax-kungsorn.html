KUNGSÖRN

Kungsörn sUAS is an advanced vtol. It is capable of launch using a slingshot system. The Kungsörn sUAS is designed for fully autonomous flight and landing with maximum endurance of 3.5 hours depending on configuration

The sUAS is equipped with an EO/IR Gimbal for intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance missions for police, military, or anti-terrorist applications, as well as civil applications such as geological surveys, terrain mapping and patrolling.

The on-board A.I integration, allows the sUAS to be used for target recognition and enhanced ISR missions on the fly, transmitting real time visual feed to the ground command center for analytics and rapid response
